Welcome to Our Website

The festival that I like the most is Diwali. We celebrate it either in October or in November. In Gujarat, it is celebrated for five days. Every house is decorated with rangoli and oil lamps. We start preparing for Diwali a few weeks in advance by cleaning our houses. Sweets and savouries are made and new clothes are bought for everyone. We celebrate it with a lot of pomp and festivity.
